--- HOOK VIDEO 1: "Dây chuyền trả góp" ---

A cinematic tracking shot following a young Vietnamese office worker through their entire day. Morning: they wake up, check phone, notification shows "Kỳ thanh toán laptop: 1.500.000đ". They get dressed, grab their motorbike keys with a tag dangling that reads "Trả góp tháng 8/12". They ride through Ho Chi Minh City streets passing billboards advertising "Trả góp 0%". At work, they open their phone banking app and we see multiple recurring payments listed. Lunch, they eat simply at a street vendor.

Evening: they arrive home to their small rental room, sit on the bed, pull out their phone. The screen fills with notification badges from payment apps, all showing red overdue alerts. Camera slowly pulls back to reveal the room is small and bare, the only expensive items are the things bought on installment: a new phone, a laptop, nice headphones, all with small price tag labels still dangling from them showing remaining balance.

Final shot: the person sits on the bed surrounded by expensive objects they technically don't own yet, the room lit only by the phone screen showing overdue payments. A subtle visual of chains made of receipt paper connecting each item back to the person.

Style: naturalistic day-in-the-life documentary feel transitioning to moody cinematic at evening. Warm morning light cooling to cold blue evening. Handheld camera for daytime, steady controlled shots for nighttime. Ambient city sounds fading to silence. Aspect ratio 16:9.

--- HOOK VIDEO 2: "Con số tăng dần" ---

A dramatic cinematic sequence of a digital debt counter displayed on a massive screen in a dark void. The counter starts at 3,000,000 (three million).

A clock nearby ticks to show one day passing. The counter clicks to 3,030,000. Another day. 3,060,000. The clicks accelerate as days fly by. One week: 3,210,000. One month: 3,900,000. The numbers grow faster and faster, the ticking becomes frantic.

Cut to a young Vietnamese person watching the counter from below, their face illuminated by the red glow of the ever-growing number. They try to push the number down with their hands but it keeps climbing. They throw money at the screen but the money burns before it reaches the counter.

Three months: 6,000,000. Six months: 15,000,000. The number is now enormous, filling the entire screen, glowing angry red. The person is tiny below it, sitting on the ground, head in their hands.

Final shot: the counter continues climbing even after the person has given up. It is autonomous, self-feeding, unstoppable. The person is now in shadow, the only light comes from the merciless red counter above.

Style: pure black void environment with the counter as the only light source. Red digital numbers casting harsh light on the person below. Progressively more dramatic with each time skip. Deep bass sound design with each number tick. Aspect ratio 16:9.
